Welcome to Roastown

Flat-Work

Enjoy a one-of-a-kind experiential dining at our Flat Wok Station. Witness as our flat wok specialist prepares a special set menu right in front of you and your party. Grab a seat and watch the exciting preparation of your steaming hot dishes, as the woks are a-fuming.
